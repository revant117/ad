What is IGNITE

After the success of the inaugural edition of IGNITE, the inter-collegiate annual scientific meet of Manipal College Of Dental Sciences, Manipal, we are organising IGNITE â€™15 from 23rd-25th April,2015 on a bigger scale. The three day event will include paper presentations, e-poster presentations, table clinic, dental debate, quiz and keynote lectures by eminent speakers More than 25 colleges from Karnataka and neighbouring states are participating in this edition of IGNITE thus making the event a small hub for dental students throughout the state. The primary aim of IGNITE is to serve as an effective inter-collegiate platform for dental students and interns, which will eventually act as a medium to provide essential practice and confidence to aspiring dentists for bigger exposures nationally and internationally. Through this fest, we also intend to bring together students/interns to facilitate exchange of knowledge, ideas and sharing of literary talents.
